1- Routing from the head to an energy cell that's [easy to get to]

Reachable rules:
1- If the head can reach the same level as the cell, it can take it.
2- If a cell is within L + 1 distance from a tile (v dist + h dist) then we can reach it (L is the snake length).

Reachable tiles:
1- The head has to be at least 1 square higher than a tile it wants to get to
2- If the tile is within L distance from a tile (v dist + h dist) then we can reach it (L is the snake length).

Last body part of each snake is impossible to ram into

[easy to get to] = within jumping height AND/OR 

Jumping is ONLY useful to attack or get energy cell

At least one body part should touch a tile

Starting from the front of each snake, we take the first body part on top of a tile as the gravity anchor. It will allows us as many moves as it is far away from the tail.

We should detect if any body part after the anchor becomes a new anchor.

If the body is more than 3 body parts, ramming into shorter snakes is valid.

Beyond a ledge without a target tile, moving up is always the way.
Try to move back in the opposite direction ASAP.

Alternate strategy for maps where the enemy snakes are within 3 squares: Ram into them and go for a draw.

Alternate strategy for maps where we get larger first or race: block entrance and roam around.
